Solving PDEs with FFT

Using the finite discrete Forier transform we can solve some problems for some partial differential equations.
  • The heat equation \(u_t-u_{xx}=0\) (heat.jl).
  • The Schrödinger evolution equation \(u_t-\sqrt{-1}u_{xx}=0\) (schroedinger.jl).
  • The linear part of the Korteweg de Vries equation \(u_t+u_{xxx}=0\) (lkdv.jl).
  • The Korteweg de Vries equation \(u_t+u_{xxx}+3(u^2)_x=0\) (kdv.jl).
  • Burgers' equation \(u_t-u_{xx}+uu_x=0\) (burgers.jl).
  • Inviscid Burgers' equation \(u_t+(u^2)_x/2=0\) (iburgers.jl).

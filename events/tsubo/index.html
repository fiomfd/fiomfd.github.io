2018 Naha Workshop on Mathematical Analysis

10:00-17:30, Saturday, December 15, 2018
Okinawa Municipality Autonomy Hall, 4F, Meeting Room No.6
  • About this workshop  
    We will have a workshop on mathematics forcusing on analysis in Downtown Naha, Okinawa Island, Japan, during the off season of typhoons. It takes 15 minutes to come to the venue from OKA Naha International Airport by monorail.

  • Access to the Venue
    It takes a few minites to walk to the venue from Asahibashi Station of Okinawa City Monorail, which is located at the same place of Naha Bus Terminal.

      Semiclassical distribution of resonances associated with an energy-level crossing

    • 15:10-16:10   Wolfram Bauer (Leibniz University of Hanover)
      On the complete integrability of the geodesic flow on pseudo-H type Lie groups

    • 16:30-17:30   Kenro Furutani (Tokyo University of Science)
      Riemannian submersion and Maslov quantization condition
